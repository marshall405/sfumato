Our Story

Sfumato Pizzeria was founded in 2012 to bring the authentic taste of Neapolitan pizza to the heart of Carnes. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best pizza experience possible, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods. We take pride in our craft and are committed to delivering a delicious, high-quality product every time.

Meet the Owners

Sfumato Pizzeria is a family-owned and operated business. Our owners, Rango and Bianca, are passionate about pizza and have dedicated their lives to perfecting the art of Neapolitan pizza-making. They bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the table, ensuring that every pizza we serve is made with love and care. We invite you to come and experience the magic of Sfumato Pizzeria for yourself!
