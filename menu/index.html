What makes Sfumato Pizza special?

Our dough is made fresh each day with only the finest flour. Crushed marzano tomatoes imported from Italy make our signature sauce truly one-of-a kind. Even our mozzarella is made fresh each day in house. Topped with the freshest hand chopped ingredients, your pizza is cooked in a 900 degree oven in just 90 seconds. It's wood-fired Neapolitan Pizza.
